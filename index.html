Team 05 - Hairless Hare Membership Application

Third Party Consultants for TapWyse Integration

Client Overview

Hairless Hare Brewery is a local craft brewery recognized for its community focused atmosphere and diverse selection of handcrafted beers. As the brewery has grown, they have sought new ways to strengthen customer loyalty and provide additional value to their regulars. One of their goals was to implement a membership based rewards program to enhance the customer experience and streamline the process of tracking “Ugly Mug Club” members, eliminating the need for manual checks.

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to research and design a custom membership application that could support Hairless Hare Brewery’s loyalty and rewards program. Our team explored the functionality of TapWyse and its potential integration with the brewery’s POS system. Acting as third-party consultants, we analyzed system capabilities, evaluated integration options, and proposed a membership solution that would be easy for both customers and staff to use during normal and peak business hours.

Sprint 1

During the first sprint, our team focused on understanding the requirements of Hairless Hare Brewery and researching potential solutions. We conducted interviews with brewery staff to gather insights into their current processes and pain points. Upon meeting with the owners, we gained a deeper understanding of their vision, and came to the conclusion that there was no need for a new POS system, but rather a custom membership application. We explored various membership application platforms and evaluated their compatibility with Arryved, their current POS system. By the end of the sprint, we had a clear understanding of the project scope and identified key features for the membership application.

Sprint 2

In the second sprint, we focused on researching the technical aspects of the proposed membership application and the current resources on the market. We discovered that TapWyse, a popular membership application platform, offered robust features and could potentially integrate with Arryved. We eventually contacted TapWyse to discuss integration possibilities and gather more information about their compatibility with Arryved. We discussed the potential pain points the Hare had with the current system and how TapWyse could address those issues. By the end of the sprint, we had a better understanding of the technical requirements for integration and were able to identify potential challenges that may arise during implementation. After gathering all the necessary information, we concluded that TapWyse could be a viable solution for Hairless Hare Brewery's membership application needs, and we were ready to move forward with the next steps in the project.

Sprint 3

During the final sprint, we focused on developing and presenting a pitch deck to Hairless Hare Brewery. We compiled all the research and findings from the previous sprints into a comprehensive presentation that outlined the benefits of integrating TapWyse with Arryved, as well as the potential challenges and solutions. We highlighted the key features of TapWyse that would enhance the customer experience and streamline the membership management process for the brewery staff. We also provided a detailed implementation plan, including timelines and resource requirements. Upon meeting with the owners, a few more questions came to light regarding practicallity and ease of use for the staff. We then reached back out to our contact at TapWyse to discuss these concerns and gather more information about the user experience for staff members. We were able to address these concerns and provide additional insights into how TapWyse could be customized to meet the specific needs of Hairless Hare Brewery. Despite reaching out, the Hare did not have the time and resources available right now to move forward with the project, but they were very appreciative of our efforts and the information we provided. We left them with a comprehensive pitch deck that they could refer back to when they are ready to implement a membership application in the future.

Our Role as Consultants

Throughout the project, we maintained open communication with Hairless Hare Brewery and provided regular updates on our progress. We were able to build a strong relationship with the brewery staff and gain valuable insights into their operations and customer base. We gained real world experience in researching and designing a custom membership application, as well as navigating the challenges of integrating with existing systems. We also developed our skills in project management, communication, and presentation, which will be valuable in our future careers as consultants. We had to be able to adapt to the changing needs and priorities of the brewery, and we were able to provide valuable insights and recommendations that could help them achieve their goals in the future. We gained valuable techniclal research and were able to relay that information to the brewery in a way that was easy for them to understand and use in their decision making process. Overall, this project was a great learning experience and we are proud of the work we accomplished as consultants for Hairless Hare Brewery. If the Hare decides to move forward with implementing a membership application in the future, we are confident that the information and recommendations we provided will be valuable in helping them make informed decisions and successfully implement a solution that meets their needs.
